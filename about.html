About

Pratik Pawar is a freelance science journalist based in Bangalore, India. His stories have appeared in Connect Magazine, Discover, Science, Science News, The Open Notebook, The Washington Post, The Wire, and ThePrint.

He is a recipient of the 2019 S. Ramaseshan Science Writing Fellowship. As a fellow, he writes news stories and profiles at Current Science, India's leading open-access research journal. He is also a recipient of the 2020 EurekAlert! Fellowship for International Science Reporters. He is a member of the National Association of Science Writers.

He graduated with a master's degree in biotechnology in 2018 and has since been on the lookout for stories about science and science policy. Alongside the written word, he has a long-standing interest in podcasting. In his free time, he can be found reading moral philosophy or listening to the latest Radiolab.
