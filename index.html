About

Hi, I'm a freelance science journalist based in Bangalore, India. My work has appeared in Connect Magazine, Discover, Science News, The Open Notebook, The Washington Post, The Wire, among other publications.

I was awarded the EurekAlert! Fellowship for International Science Reporters in early 2020. I am also a recipient of the S. Ramaseshan Science Writing Fellowship awarded by the Indian Academy of Sciences.

I cover stories about ecology, genetics, and lately, infectious diseases. If you'd like to chat, please drop me an email.
